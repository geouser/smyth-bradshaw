who we are?

A Boutique Search and HR Company

Smyth and Bradshaw is a boutique search and HR Firm that offers a unique and innovative approach to the search process.

SWith an unwavering passion for people and commitment to unparalleled service, Smyth and Bradshaw has successfully been matching people to jobs for the past 20 years.

In 2014 Smyth and Bradshaw arrived to Dubai with the goal of serving as an intermediary between clients and candidates to help both parties better meet their needs.

A Passion for People

As a firm, our priority lies in providing our clients and candidates with a service that is second to none.

We pride ourselves on offering personal, one to one consultation that is specifically tailored to ensure we satisfy each and every need you put forth. For every company that approaches us, we begin the search process by personally visiting them to gain a precise understanding of their needs. By working with Smyth and Bradshaw you can ensure that the search process will be taken out of your hands completely and left entirely up to us.

A Nuanced Approach

Smyth and Bradshaw prides itself on its honesty, integrity and reliability. As a firm we operate on the principle of quality rather than quantity.

Our satisfaction comes from matching the perfect client to the perfect job as opposed to simply filling positions.

At Smyth and Bradshaw we understand that a job is so much more than a means of employment, it has the power to shape a person’s life, family, career, and future and that is why we are committed to achieving excellence in what we do.
