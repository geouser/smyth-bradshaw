Vacancy

Regional Account Manager Leading paid Advertising Marketing Automation company Dubai, UAE

Details

The Company

Our client is a global leader in Social Media Automation and Optimization. They have offices all over the world and work with over 400 clients and are looking to expand their operations to the Middle East. They have already closed a number of deals with some large customers in the region and are looking for someone to manage these clients and bring in some new accounts.

The Role

This is a hands on role where you will be proactive in identifying potential clients, meeting them and close business. This is an individual contribution position and you will be the first resource for the company to be based in the region so you must be capable of managing yourself and have a driven, hunter-type personality. This is a quota carrying sales role so its important that you have a track record of successful solution selling.

Key Responsibilities

  • Identify the right clients and contacts and build a relationship with them.
  • Develop and manage top customer relationships and drive new sales by managing customer trials.
  • Develop vertical expertise (e.g. travel, mobile apps, e-commerce etc.)
  • Provide best practices, data insights, and product knowledge to create business solutions for clients.
  • Up-sell new features and increase product adoption.
  • Collaborate with customers and product development team to design new product features.

The Candidate

This role requires a driven individual who enjoys meeting with customers and has a strong understanding of the markets in the region. You should have a genuine interest in Online Marketing/B2B sales and have experience in new business acquisition and Online Marketing. You should be an independent thinker and be able to make high level decisions on a regular basis.

Skills & Qualifications

Analytical and technical mindset.
A background in technology.
Proven experience in sales.
Quick and independent thinker.
